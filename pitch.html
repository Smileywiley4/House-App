Core Features

Built for buyers, upgradable for professionals.

🔍 Property search

Enter any address. Get property details, proximity to amenities, walk scores, school ratings — cached for consistency.

⚖️ Weighted scoring

30+ categories: roof quality, school district, commute. Set importance (0–10) and score each property. Get a weighted total out of 100.

📊 Side-by-side comparison

Compare 2+ properties. Free plan: up to 2; Premium: 3+. View as columns, cards, or table.

✨ AI features

AI property insights, auto-score suggestions, and For You recommendations. Premium & Realtor only.

🏷️ For Sale badge

Properties publicly listed for sale show a badge. Data aligned with major listing sites.

👔 Realtor portal

Client management, private listings, share scorecards. Realtor plan only.
